        Notes:

        1

        (from gangs can mkhas dbang rim byon gyi rnam thar mdor bsdusbo-x-ewts @ v. 2, pp.104-112)

        biography
        dates: 1308-1363
        en

        2

        (from snga 'gyur rdzogs chen chos 'byung chen mobo-x-ewts @ 251)

        biodata

        Subsequent main lineage holders include lha btsun chen po/ gter chen 'gyur med rdo rje/ 'bri gung rin chen phun tshogs/ rig 'dzin 'jigs med gling pa/ mi 'gyur nam mkha'i rdo rje/ 'jam dbyangs mkhyen brtse'i dbang po

        Among them, 'jigs med gling pa received the nye brgyud via direct vision of klong chen pa. 'jigs med gling pa passed his lineage to rdo grub phrin las 'od zer, who passed it to mi 'gyur nam mkha' rdo rje, who in turn had a vision of klong chen pa and received the yang tig from him.
        en

        3

        (from po ta lar bzhugs pa'i rnying ma'i gsung 'bum dkar chagbo-x-ewts @ p. 6)

        4

        (from ming mdzodbo-x-ewts @ p. 77)

        5

        Teachers (not recorded):
        Slob-dpon Dbang-ye, Ston-tshul, Khro-phu-ba, Rin-po-che Za-lung-pa (Zhang Tshal-pa'i Gdams ngag and Rgod-tshang-pa's teachings)
        1319. Takes rab-byung vows from the abbot of Bsam-yas Bsam-grub-rin-chen.
        1326. Enrolls in Gsang-phu Ne'u-thog for formal studies with Mkhan-rabs 15 Slob-dpon Btsan-rgod-pa and Mkhan-rabs 16 Bla-brang Chos-dpal-rgyal-mtshan.
        1338. Comes to Snye-phu Shug-gseb and teaches Snying thig.
        en

        6

        L. v.d. Kuijp has verified that the date of death of the subject was January, 1364. Also the unrecorded teacher Ston tshul may have been Btsun pa Ston tshul, Sa-skya scholar and author of at least two works on Tshad ma.en
